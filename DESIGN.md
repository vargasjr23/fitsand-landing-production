# Design System: Fitsand Landing
**Project ID:** projects/2845711303080961440

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The identity is bright, structured, and modern, blending utilitarian layout principles with energetic color accents. The atmosphere is grounded yet dynamic, leveraging the architectural rigidity of Space Grotesk against high-saturation, warm yellow highlights to draw the user's eye confidently through the hierarchy. It embraces a light, airy canvas that feels like a premium editorial spread.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Sunlit Gold (#e7b923):** Used for primary actions, critical highlights, floating elements, and key interactive CTA buttons. Drives attention and action.
- **Crisp Off-White/Paper (Light Mode Background):** Serves as the global backdrop, keeping the interface airy and breathable without the harshness of pure white.
- **Deep Charcoal/Black (Primary Text):** Used for high-contrast primary typography (headings and body) to ensure maximum readability against light surfaces and golden highlights.
- **Subtle Surface Tones (Light Gray/Offset White):** Used for nested containers or soft borders to define structural sections and feature cards without using heavy dividing lines.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Global Font:** `Space Grotesk` is used exclusively across the experience.
- **Headings:** Bold, commanding, and rigid. Space Grotesk's slight geometric quirks add character to large display text, which should be set with tight tracking (`tracking-tight`) to feel locked-in and structural.
- **Body & Labels:** Clean and highly legible. Use regular or medium weights with breathable line height (e.g., `leading-relaxed`) to balance and contrast the dense headings.

## 4. Component Stylings
- **Buttons:** Bold, energetic blocks featuring the Sunlit Gold (`#e7b923`) background and dark text for contrast. They utilize moderately rounded corners (`ROUND_FOUR` / `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`) to slightly soften the strict geometric typography.
- **Cards/Containers:** Elevated gently from the background with consistent `ROUND_FOUR` (`rounded-xl` / `rounded-2xl`) border radii. Shadows are soft, diffused, and ambient to maintain the light, modern aesthetic without feeling heavy or dated.
- **Inputs/Forms:** Clean, utilitarian fields. Borders are subtle, drawing focus via a Sunlit Gold highlight or ring only upon user focus/interaction.

## 5. Layout Principles
The layout prioritizes expansive, desktop-first panoramic viewing with clear, asymmetrical balance. Whitespace is generous but fiercely organized, allowing the strict geometry of Space Grotesk and the energetic punch of the primary gold color to breathe, directing the user seamlessly down the page. Sections seamlessly flow into one another using tonal shifts rather than hard borders.
